Administer intravenous over 15 minutes

10 mg/ml (intravenous)

Assess for signs of pain. Monitor temperature. Assess liver function. Serum acetaminophen concentration is obtained only to assess toxicity.

Injection site events (pain and site reactions) and vomiting occur with IV acetaminophen. Rash, fever, thrombocytopenia, leukopenia, and neutropenia. Serious skin reactions have been reported from patients who were rechallenged with acetaminophen and had a recurrence of a serious skin reaction.
